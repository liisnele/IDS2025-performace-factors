StudentPerformanceFactors.csv file column descriptions:

Hours_Studied: Number of study hours per week
Attendance: Percentage of attendance throughout the term
Parental_Involvement: Level of parental support in academics (Low, Medium, High)
Access_to_Resources: Availability of academic Resources like books or internet (Low, Medium, High)
Extracurricular_Activities: Whether the student takes part in extracurricular activities (Yes, No)
Sleep_Hours: Average hours of sleep per day
Previos_Scores: Average of previous academic scores
Motivation_Level: Student's morivation level (Low, Medium, High)
Internet_Access: Whether the student has Access to the internet (Yes, No)
Tutoring_Sessions: Number of tutoring sessions per week
Family_Income: Family income level (Low, Medium, High)
Teacher_Quality: Overall perceived teacher quality (Low, Medium, High)
School_Type: Type of school (Public, Private)
Peer_Influence: Influence of peers on academic performance (Negative, Neutral, Positive)
Physical_Activity: Hours of physical activity per week
Learning_Disability: Presence of any learning disabilities (Yes, No)
Parental_Education_Level: Highest education level of parents (High School, College, Postgraduate)
Distance_from_Home: Distance between home and school (Near, Moderate, Far)
Gender: Student’s gender (Male, Female)
Exam_Score: Final exam score (0–100) — the target variable
